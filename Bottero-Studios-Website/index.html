Bottero Discord Bot

This was our first project marking the start of Bottero Studios. We made a simple Discord Bot that performs various commands based on the users input. The discord bot includes chat and music commands and has a great potential for future improvements.

SignSpeak

This project had two parts. The first part was a robotic hand that could perform signs of the ASL alphabet. The second part was a website that allowed users to perform hand signs that would be translated into English. This project won us $320 as the first place prize for HackMCST.

IMA

IMA, other wise known as Immediate Medical Assisstant, is a AI medical assistant that helps you with your medical needs. You enter your symptoms with text or voice input and it gives you a diagnosis along with a solution.

Grocer-Ease

Grocer-Ease is a web application created to help everyday people purchase healthy and budget friendly groceries in a time of rising inflation. It contains features to located food pantries, list grocery items with a built-in budgeting feature, and a barcode scanner for grocery items to fetch nutritional information.
