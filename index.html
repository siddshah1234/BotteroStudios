Our Goal

What We're Aiming to Accomplish

We are Bottero Studios. We plan to create diverse and creative projects to entertain and help the public. Our goal is to benefit our community through our various services. We hope to inspire others to create and develop projects of their own with our innovative ideas.

Bottero Discord Bot

This was our first project, marking the start of Bottero Studios. We built a Discord bot that performs various commands based on user input. It includes chat and music commands and has great potential for future improvements.

SignSpeak

This project had two parts. The first was a robotic hand that could perform signs of the ASL alphabet. The second was a website that allowed users to perform hand signs that would be translated into English. This project won us $320 as the first-place prize at HackMCST.
